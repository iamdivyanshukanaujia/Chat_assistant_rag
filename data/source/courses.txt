🎓 B.TECH – COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING (CSE)
Semester 1

CS100 – Intro to Computational Thinking (4 credits)
Department: CSE
Prerequisites: None
Description: Basics of algorithms, problem solving, and programming logic using Python.

CS101 – Foundations of Programming (4 credits)
Fundamentals of programming, control structures, functions, and data types.

CS102 – Discrete Mathematics (3 credits)
Logic, sets, relations, combinatorics, graphs, and fundamental proof techniques.

CS110 – Digital Logic and Systems (3 credits)
Binary systems, logic gates, Boolean algebra, and combinational circuits.

CS111 – Engineering Mathematics I (3 credits)
Covers calculus, differential equations, and linear algebra basics.

Semester 2

CS120 – Object-Oriented Programming (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS101
Covers OOP principles, classes, inheritance, and design patterns.

CS121 – Data Structures (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS101
Includes arrays, lists, stacks, queues, trees, hashing, and complexity analysis.

CS122 – Computer Organization (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS110
CPU architecture, memory hierarchy, instruction sets, and assembly basics.

CS130 – Software Engineering I (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS101
SDLC, UML, requirements analysis, and introductory testing.

CS131 – Probability & Statistics for CS (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS102
Probability, distributions, estimations, and hypothesis testing.

Semester 3

CS200 – Algorithms I (4 credits)
Prerequisites: CS121, CS122
Sorting, searching, divide and conquer, and dynamic programming.

CS201 – Theory of Computation (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS102
Automata, languages, Turing machines, decidability, and complexity.

CS202 – Operating Systems I (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS122
Processes, threads, scheduling, concurrency, and memory management.

CS210 – Database Systems (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS121
Relational algebra, SQL, schema design, indexing, and transactions.

CS211 – Computer Networks I (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS122
OSI model, TCP/IP, routing, and socket programming.

Semester 4

CS220 – Algorithms II (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS200
Advanced algorithms, graph algorithms, and approximation methods.

CS221 – Software Engineering II (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS130
Architecture patterns, CI/CD, testing strategies, and project management.

CS222 – Machine Learning Basics (4 credits)
Prerequisites: CS131, CS121
Supervised/unsupervised learning, evaluation, and ML pipelines.

CS230 – Web Technologies (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS120
Frontend/backend fundamentals, REST APIs, and web deployment.

CS231 – Human-Computer Interaction (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS130
UI/UX principles, usability testing, and prototyping.

Semester 5

CS300 – Distributed Systems (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS202
Distributed computing, consensus algorithms, fault tolerance, and scalability.

CS301 – Compiler Design (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS200
Lexing, parsing, symbol tables, IR, optimizations, and code generation.

CS302 – Database Internals (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS210
Storage engines, query optimizations, indexing, and transactions.

CS310 – Advanced Networks (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS211
Advanced routing, network security, SDN, and wireless networking.

CS311 – Information Retrieval (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS210
Search engines, indexing, ranking, and evaluation.

Semester 6

CS320 – AI Foundations (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS222
Search, reasoning, knowledge representation, and planning.

CS321 – Deep Learning (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS222
Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, optimization, and DL training pipelines.

CS322 – Cloud Computing (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS300
Virtualization, containers, cloud services, orchestration.

CS330 – Security & Privacy (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS202
Cryptography basics, secure coding, threat modeling, privacy.

CS331 – Mobile App Development (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS230
Frameworks, offline-first design, platform-specific optimizations.

Semester 7

CS400 – Capstone Project I (4 credits)
Prerequisites: CS221, CS321
Project proposal, planning, initial implementation.

CS401 – Elective: Advanced Algorithms (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS220
Randomized, streaming, and parallel algorithms.

CS402 – Elective: NLP (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS321
Text processing, embeddings, sequence models.

Semester 8

CS410 – Internship / Industrial Training (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS400
Industrial experience and final report.

CS411 – Capstone Project II (4 credits)
Prerequisite: CS400
Project completion, evaluation, and demonstration.

CS412 – Elective: Blockchain Systems (3 credits)
Prerequisite: CS300
Distributed ledgers, consensus, smart contracts.
