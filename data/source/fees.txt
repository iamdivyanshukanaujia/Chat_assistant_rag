🎓 UNIVERSITY FEE STRUCTURE AND SCHOLARSHIP POLICIES

This document summarizes annual tuition fees, hostel charges, exam fees per semester, miscellaneous fees, scholarship eligibility, and refund rules for all BTech, MTech, and PhD programs.
Domestic and international students have different fee categories.

🧑‍🎓 B.TECH FEE STRUCTURE
B.Tech – Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
Domestic Students

Tuition fee per year: ₹1,45,000

Hostel fee per year: ₹60,000

Exam fee per semester: ₹5,000

Miscellaneous fee: ₹10,000

Scholarship Criteria:
Top 10% performers or JEE Rank < 5000. Need-based scholarships available.

Refund Policy:
100% refund before semester starts; 50% refund if withdrawn within first 2 weeks.

International Students

Tuition fee per year: ₹3,20,000

Hostel fee per year: ₹70,000

Exam fee per semester: ₹9,000

Miscellaneous fee: ₹15,000

Scholarship Criteria: Merit-based only; limited quota.

Refund Policy: No refund after course registration.
