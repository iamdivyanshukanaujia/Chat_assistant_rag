📜 General Hostel Rules

Students must carry their hostel ID cards at all times.

Keep hostel premises clean and litter-free.

Smoking, alcohol, and drugs are strictly prohibited.

No loud noise or music after 10:00 PM (Quiet Hours).

Property damage must be reported; fines apply.

Electric stoves, heaters, and high-wattage appliances are not allowed.

Gambling, ragging, or harassment is strictly prohibited.
