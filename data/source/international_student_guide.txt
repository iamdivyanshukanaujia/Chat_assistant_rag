✈️ PRE-ARRIVAL REQUIREMENTS
Required Documents to Carry

International students must bring:

Valid Passport (with at least 6 months validity)

Student Visa (S-1 category recommended)

University Admission Letter

Fee Payment Acknowledgment

Vaccination Certificates (if required)

10–12 Passport-size photographs

Recommended Items

Local currency (₹5,000–₹10,000 for immediate expenses)

Universal power adapters

Basic medicines

Health insurance documentation

Pre-Arrival Orientation

A mandatory online orientation session is conducted one week before the reporting date.

🛂 VISA & FRRO REGULATIONS
Visa Requirements

All international students must hold a valid Student Visa (S-1).

Apply for visa renewal at least 60 days before expiry.

The university assists with documentation for renewal.

FRRO Registration (Mandatory)

International students must register with FRRO within 14 days of arrival in India.

Documents required for FRRO registration:

Passport and Student Visa

University Admission Letter

Proof of Address (Hostel)

Passport photographs

Undertaking Form

The International Relations Office supports students in scheduling FRRO appointments.
