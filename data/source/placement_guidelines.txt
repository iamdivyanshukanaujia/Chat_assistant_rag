🎓 ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Minimum CGPA Required

BTech: 6.0

MTech: 6.5

PhD: No CGPA requirement — must be in good academic standing

Attendance Requirement

Minimum 75% attendance in all registered courses.

Students with active backlogs are NOT eligible for placements.

Exception: Final semester backlog may be allowed only for internship placements.

Disciplinary Record

Students with disciplinary issues in the past 12 months are debarred from placements.

📜 PLACEMENT RULES & CODE OF CONDUCT
Rules Students Must Follow

Once selected and offer accepted → student cannot sit for further placements.

Attendance at company PPT sessions is mandatory.

Absenteeism at any stage after applying may lead to debarment.

Confidential company materials must not be shared.

Maintain professional behavior throughout the process.

Penalties

No-show for test: Debarred for the rest of the semester

Fake resume information: Permanent debarment

Interview misconduct: Reported to disciplinary committee

Withdrawing after final selection: Blacklisted from placement cell
